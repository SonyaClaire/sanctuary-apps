Role-Indexed Synastry — Formal Definition
RIS is an interpretive methodology for relationship astrology in which the relationship role assigned to the person being read for is established as the primary interpretive context before any planetary data is analyzed.
The role determines: (a) which planets and points are foregrounded; (b) how aspects and overlays are weighted; (c) which houses are prioritized; (d) the core question the chart is being asked to answer.
The Three Psychological Pillars
Schema Theory (Fiske & Taylor) — cognitive frameworks filter and interpret incoming relational data based on established role expectations
Social Categorization Theory (Turner 1987) — role-based categorization precedes and shapes interpersonal perception
Attachment Theory (Bowlby / Hazan & Shaver) — relational roles activate distinct attachment behavioral systems with different planetary correlates
The Six Role Lenses
Romantic
Venus, Mars, 5th/7th/8th house. Desire, attraction, long-term union potential.
Family
Moon, Saturn, 4th/10th house. Attachment patterns, obligation, roots, care.
Friend / Chosen Family
Mercury, Uranus, 3rd/11th house. Resonance, communication, chosen bonds.
Mentor / Authority
Jupiter, Saturn, 9th/10th house. Growth, hierarchy, wisdom transmission.
Rival / Adversary
Mars, Pluto, 1st/7th/12th house. Opposition, power dynamics, shadow work.
Karmic / Past Life
Nodes (NN/SN), Saturn, Chiron, 12th house. Unfinished patterns, soul contracts.
Ophiuchus Protocol
Any planet between 27° Scorpio and 27° Sagittarius is flagged. Read both adjacent sign interpretations alongside the Asclepius archetype (healer, serpent-bearer, boundary between life and death). Observe which the subject recognizes. Record result.
Statistical Protocol — RIS Pilot Study
OSF pre-registration required before collecting any participant data (non-negotiable)
Minimum n≥30 per role category before statistical analysis
Wilcoxon signed-rank test with Bonferroni-corrected p<0.017 threshold
Unknown birth time protocol: noon default with flagged uncertainty, exclude from house analysis
Barnum control required: matched generic reading for each RIS reading
